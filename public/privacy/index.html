Privacy

This is a personal portfolio. Please do not submit passwords, financial details, government IDs, or other confidential information through AdrAI or the contact form.

AdrAI

AdrAI answers questions using the portfolio’s public content. When its server-side AI provider is enabled, your question and the minimum relevant portfolio context are sent to that provider to generate a response. The feature can fall back to local portfolio responses when the provider is unavailable. Chat messages are kept in your browser for the current experience and should not be treated as a private communication channel.

Contact

The contact form uses the name, email address, and message you provide solely to prepare and deliver your inquiry. If delivery is unavailable, the portfolio offers an email-draft fallback instead.

Visits and diagnostics

The Total Visits display is incremented by a server endpoint with rate limiting; the browser does not write the count directly. Basic anonymous usage events may be used to understand portfolio navigation. This site does not sell personal information.

Your choices

You can avoid AdrAI and the contact form entirely. For privacy questions or a deletion request relating to a message you sent, email dagsmagalona@gmail.com.
